Long-time IU fan to fulfill lifelong dream with Rose Bowl trip

by WSBT 22

Tue, December 30, 2025 at 7:32 AM
Updated Tue, December 30, 2025 at 10:43 AM
3VIEW ALL PHOTOS

A long-time Indiana football fan is headed to the Rose Bowl. (File)

A long-time Indiana football fan is headed to the Rose Bowl.

Bill Murphy, who lives to the east of Indianapolis, has been an IU season ticket holder for 66 years!

The vast majority of those have been losing seasons with a few bright spots, few and far between.

Indiana's only previous Rose Bowl experience was in 1968, which they lost.

For Murphy, this trip is the chance to fulfill a lifelong dream.

He was 15 the last time IU headed to California for the Rose Bowl and wasn't allowed to go.

IU plays Alabama on New Year's Day in the Rose Bowl, which Murphy will be at!
